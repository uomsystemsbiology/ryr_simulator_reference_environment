Double-click the script below and select 'Execute in Terminal' to run
the RyR cluster simulator.  Results are described in:
   Rajagopal, V., Bass, G., Walker, C. G., Crossman, D. J., Petzer, 
   A., Hickey, A., ... & Soeller, C. (2015). Examination of the 
   Effects of Heterogeneous Organization of RyR Clusters, Myofibrils
   and Mitochondria on Ca 2+ Release Patterns in Cardiomyocytes. 
   PLOS Comput Biol, 11(9), e1004417.
